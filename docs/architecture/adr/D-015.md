# D-015 — Capture certification tiers

Status: proposed
Date: 2026-04-25
Authors: Blake
Supersedes: none

## Context

Venue captures arrive on a wide quality spectrum. A Trades Hall LiDAR scan with a Z+F IMAGER 5016 is millimetre-accurate; a phone scan with Scaniverse is appearance-only. Pricing, marketing, operational use, and the spatial confidence budget (D-011) all depend on knowing which tier a venue's capture is. Without an explicit ladder, we will be tempted (or asked) to claim Pro3 captures are "measurement grade" when they are not — that path leads to load-bearing planner errors, refunds, and reputational loss. The capture market also has a clear three-phase rollout pattern: own the first dozen venues, then partner, then open the long tail.

## Decision

Capture certification ladder:

- **Bronze** — phone, 360 camera, Scaniverse. Tier: `APPEARANCE_ONLY`.
- **Silver** — Pro3 with control distances. Tier: `LAYOUT_GRADE_5CM`.
- **Gold** — Leica BLK360 G2, Leica RTC360. Tier: `OPS_GRADE_2CM`.
- **Black Label** — RTC360 + Phase One, Z+F IMAGER 5016. Tier: `SURVEY_GRADE_1CM`.

Three-phase rollout:

1. **Months 0–6:** own capture for 10–15 hero venues (Trades Hall and similar). End-to-end ownership lets us tune the pipeline without partner variability.
2. **Months 6–18:** Certified Capture Partner network. Partners pay/share via a 15–25% platform fee on bookings driven through Venviewer. Each partner certifies to a tier; their captures inherit that tier in the AssetVersion bundle (D-014).
3. **Months 18+:** consumer self-serve, locked to `APPEARANCE_ONLY` — anyone can capture their own venue with a phone, but those captures cannot use the Measure tool (D-011) and cannot produce operational artifacts (D-012).

Pro3 cannot honestly be marketed as measurement-grade for survey-critical use cases. The marketing surface must reflect this; the QA model in D-011 enforces it.

## Consequences

Pricing tiers can be aligned to capture tier (Black Label captures justify premium pricing). Sales conversations have a concrete ladder rather than vague "professional capture" language. The spatial confidence budget (D-011) gets its tier input directly from the AssetVersion bundle (D-014). Partner onboarding has a documented ladder to certify to.

## Alternatives considered

- **No certification ladder** — rejected. We will be tempted to overstate capture quality; planners will be misled.
- **Two tiers (consumer / professional)** — rejected. Coarser than the actual market: BLK360 and Pro3 are very different, and Z+F is in a different league again.
- **Uncapped consumer self-serve (let phone scans use Measure)** — rejected. Phone scans aren't 5 cm accurate; allowing measurements would propagate silent error.
