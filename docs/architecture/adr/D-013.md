# D-013 — Format strategy and standards

Status: proposed
Date: 2026-04-25
Authors: Blake
Supersedes: none

## Context

Standards for 3D capture, splat distribution, and venue/event metadata are stabilizing in 2026. Picking the wrong horse costs us a multi-year format migration; refusing to pick costs interoperability with capture vendors, AV/lighting tooling, and downstream archive systems. The synthesis identified four standards bodies that matter for Venviewer:

- **Khronos glTF** — KHR_gaussian_splatting reached RC in February 2026; ratification expected Q2 2026 with ~25% slip risk per Claude-DR. KHR_gaussian_splatting_compression_spz_2 is the companion compression extension.
- **OpenUSD** — v26.03 added `UsdVolParticleField3DGaussianSplat`, the splat schema mirror.
- **IFC 4.3** — the building-information-modeling standard that operational metadata (BEO, capacities, fire compliance) should align with for downstream interoperability.
- **MVR / GDTF** — entertainment industry standard for AV and lighting plots; MVR uses glTF as its 3D payload, so support is near-free once we are glTF-native.

## Decision

- **3D delivery:** pin to glTF + KHR_gaussian_splatting + KHR_gaussian_splatting_compression_spz_2 once the extensions are ratified. Until then, ship our internal SPZ format (D-001) inside a glTF wrapper to ease the cutover.
- **VSIR-0 mirror:** the VSIR-0 graph (D-009) mirrors `UsdVolParticleField3DGaussianSplat` semantics so a future USD adapter is a translation, not a rewrite.
- **Event/BEO sidecar:** layer event and BEO data as JSON sidecar following IFC 4.3 conventions, so customer architects, fire officers, and BIM-aware vendors can consume it.
- **AV / lighting:** support MVR / GDTF import for AV plots; the glTF-native posture makes this cheap.
- **Don't invent a "VSP" published standard** of our own until 50+ certified venues exist (per Claude-DR §10). Until then, internal-only.
- **Khronos contributor membership** ($50k/yr): defer until revenue justifies the seat at the table.

## Consequences

Capture vendors and downstream BIM/AV tooling have a documented integration surface. We avoid bespoke-format lock-in. The OpenUSD mirror keeps an exit option open if the industry coalesces on USD over glTF. Event-data interop matches the IFC convention used by the building/architectural side of customer organisations.

## Alternatives considered

- **Custom format only ("VSP")** — rejected for now. Vendor ecosystem rejects custom formats until the platform has scale (50+ venues per Claude-DR §10).
- **OpenUSD primary, glTF secondary** — rejected. glTF has the splat extension first; USD support is an added mirror, not a primary.
- **Defer all format decisions until 2027 ratification dust settles** — rejected. Interop blockers happen now, not in 2027.
