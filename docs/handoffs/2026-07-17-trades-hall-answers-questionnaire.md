# The Answers — questions for the Trades Hall events team

**Date:** 2026-07-17
**Purpose:** venviewer.com's remaining content gap is operational truth —
the questions every couple and organiser asks before enquiring. This
questionnaire gathers it in one sitting. Whatever the team answers gets
published **verbatim as venue truth** (same law as capacities and rates:
figures and policies come only from the venue, never invented, pinned by
tests). Unanswered questions simply don't appear on the page — nothing
gets guessed.

**How to use:** forward to the events team (or walk it through on a call —
~20 minutes). Short answers are fine; "it depends — ask us" is a
publishable answer too.

---

## 1 · Ceremonies and licensing

1. Is Trades Hall licensed for civil marriage and civil partnership
   ceremonies? If so, which rooms are covered by the licence?
2. For each licensed room: the maximum seated for a ceremony?
3. Can couples hold a ceremony and reception in different rooms on the
   same day (e.g., ceremony in the Robert Adam Room, dinner in the Grand
   Hall)?

## 2 · What each wedding package includes

For each published package (Wedding Breakfast & Evening Reception ·
Twilight Wedding · Evening Reception · Ceremony only from 2027/28):

4. Which rooms, and for what hours?
5. What furniture and linen is included (tables, chairs, cloths)?
6. Is staffing included (event manager on the day, bar staff, cloakroom)?
7. Are candles / open flames permitted? Confetti (real, dried, none)?
8. When can suppliers get in to set up, and when must everything be out?

## 3 · Catering and drinks

9. Is catering in-house, from an approved list, or open to any caterer?
   (If a list: who's on it?)
10. Can guests' own drinks be brought in? Corkage terms?
11. What kitchen facilities exist for external caterers?

## 4 · Access and accessibility

12. Is there step-free access into the building and to each event room?
13. Is there a lift, and which floors does it serve?
14. Accessible WCs — where?
15. Is there a hearing loop or other assistive provision?
16. Accessible parking or drop-off arrangements at the door?

## 5 · Getting there

17. Nearest car parks (names), and typical evening cost if known?
18. Best drop-off point for taxis and cars on Glassford Street?
19. Nearest rail/subway stops worth naming?

## 6 · The practical evening

20. Music curfew time, and any sound limits?
21. Latest guest departure time?
22. Can deliveries arrive the day before? Is overnight storage possible?
23. Smoking area?
24. Do suppliers need public liability insurance (what cover)?

## 7 · Money and process

25. Deposit to secure a date, and the payment schedule after that?
26. Cancellation and date-change terms, in a sentence each?
27. Do you offer provisional holds? How long do they last?
28. What response time can we promise on the site for enquiries
    (e.g., "we reply within one working day")?

## 8 · Proof

29. May we quote reviews couples have left (with first names), or should
    we link to a review platform?
30. Are there past-event photographs the venue owns that we may publish
    with credit?
31. Any preferred-supplier relationships worth naming on the page?

---

*Answers land in `packages/web/src/lib/trades-hall-venue-truth.ts` (or a
sibling operational-truth module), rendered in a new Answers section and
