## Programme reporting

Each weekly readout reports:

- gates changed and evidence links;
- hypotheses confirmed/falsified/reopened;
- metrics and failures by representation;
- rights/licence changes;
- actual versus planned compute/cost;
- new raw-data custody risks;
- operator time and recapture value;
- next cheapest decisive tests.

Do not report lines of code, asset bytes or splat count as outcome metrics.
